Mission

Starting With Rural India

Tatwamasi's mission is to empower needy and poor youth, children
and community realise their true potential by providing a platform
that encompasses education, healthcare, sports and environmental initiatives. Arijit Singh has envisioned Tatwamasi with a passion for
giving back to the community.
